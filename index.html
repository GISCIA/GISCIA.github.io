Who are we?

GISCIA is a research lab located in Lima, Peru. We are known for our research papers, the numerous awards we have received nationally, and for being very selective with our members. All of our members (so far) are from Universidad Nacional de Ingenieria (UNI). We have 2 main areas of research : Control Systems and Artificial Intelligence
